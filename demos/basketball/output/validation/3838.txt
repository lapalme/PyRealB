The Cleveland Cavaliers defeated the Miami Heat, 91-89, at Quicken Loans Arena on

determine a winner, as it was within a five-point margin for the entirety of the second
half. Three-point shooting was Miami's downfall, as they went just 3-of-28 from long

James, as he collected 24 points, 11 rebounds and five assists. Isaiah Thomas shot just

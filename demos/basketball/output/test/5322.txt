encounter although judging how the game happened, the Blazers always looked on top of the
game. Portland jumped to a 32-25 lead in the first quarter and they were set to cruise
their way, but Charlotte responded and won both the second and third quarters, 25-24 and
30-25, so the fourth quarter began with a one-point differential. But the Blazers stepped
it up and closed things out with a strong 37-28 period that gave the Western Conference
franchise their sixth win in their last seven games. Damian Lillard had a strong game for
the Blazers and filled the stat sheet admirably, since he ended with 23 points, three
rebounds, two assists and two steals. However, the best player was Jusuf Nurkic since he
ended with 26 points, 15 rebounds, six assists and two steals. Al-Farouq Aminu had 14
points with six rebounds and four dimes. Rodney Hood impressed and was the top scorer
despite coming off the bench thanks to his 27 points. Kemba Walker delivered a double-
double thanks to 18 points and 12 rebounds in this game, but it wasn't enough for the
Hornets. Miles Bridges was the only other starter who scored in double digits since he had
